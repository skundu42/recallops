# Invoicing

## Invoice schedule

Invoices for annual plans are generated on the subscription anniversary date.
Monthly plans invoice on the same calendar day each month; if the anniversary day
does not exist in a month (for example the 31st), the invoice is issued on the last
day of that month. All invoices are due net 30.

## Payment methods

We accept major credit cards, ACH debit for US accounts, and SEPA direct debit for
EU accounts. Wire transfer is available only for invoices above 10,000 USD and must
be arranged with the finance desk. Purchase orders can be referenced on the invoice
but do not change payment terms.

## Late payments

Unpaid invoices accrue a late fee of 1.5% per month after the due date. Accounts
with invoices more than 60 days overdue are downgraded to read-only mode until the
balance is settled. Downgrade never deletes customer data.

## Tax handling

Sales tax, VAT, and GST are calculated at invoice time based on the billing address
on file. Customers with a valid exemption certificate should upload it in the
account console before the invoice date; exemptions are not applied retroactively.

## Invoice disputes

Disputes must be raised within 15 days of the invoice date via a billing ticket.
Disputed line items are paused from collection while under review; undisputed line
items remain due on the original schedule.
