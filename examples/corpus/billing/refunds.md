# Refund Policy

## Annual plan refunds

Customers on annual plans may request a full refund within 30 days of the initial
purchase date. After the 30-day window closes, refunds for annual plans are prorated
to the nearest whole month remaining on the subscription term. Proration is computed
from the cancellation effective date, not the request date. Refunds are always issued
to the original payment method; we never issue refunds to a different card or by
bank transfer.

## Monthly plan refunds

Monthly subscriptions are not refundable once a billing cycle has started. Customers
who cancel a monthly plan retain access until the end of the paid period. As a
goodwill exception, a support lead may approve one courtesy refund per customer
lifetime for a monthly charge, capped at one month of fees.

## Non-refundable items

Usage-based overage charges, one-time onboarding fees, and marketplace add-on
purchases are non-refundable in all cases. Taxes collected are refunded only where
the tax authority permits a correction filing.

## How to request a refund

Open a billing ticket from the account console and include the invoice number.
Refund requests are processed within 5 business days. If a refund is approved, the
credit typically appears on the customer's statement within 5 to 10 business days
depending on the issuing bank.

## Chargebacks

If a customer files a chargeback instead of requesting a refund, the account is
automatically suspended pending resolution. We contest chargebacks for charges that
match signed order forms. Reversing a chargeback restores the account within 24 hours.
