# Data Processing Addendum

## Roles of the parties

For customer content, the customer is the data controller and we act as the data
processor. For account and billing records, we act as an independent controller.
The DPA incorporates the EU Standard Contractual Clauses module two for
controller-to-processor transfers.

## Subprocessors

A current list of subprocessors is maintained at the trust portal. We provide 30
days advance notice of new subprocessors by email to the designated privacy
contact; customers may object on reasonable data protection grounds within that
window, and unresolved objections permit termination of the affected service.

## Data retention and deletion

Customer content is deleted within 30 days of account termination, and backups
expire within 90 days. Deletion certificates are available on request for
Enterprise customers. Aggregated, de-identified telemetry may be retained.

## Security measures

Annex II commitments include encryption in transit and at rest, SOC 2 Type II
audits, annual penetration testing, and background checks for staff with production
access. Customers may audit once per year with 30 days notice, or rely on the
audit reports.

## Breach notification

We notify the designated privacy contact without undue delay and within 72 hours
of confirming a personal data breach, including the nature of the breach, affected
categories, and mitigation steps taken.
