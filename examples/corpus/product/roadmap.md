# Product Roadmap

## Now: reliability quarter

The current quarter is dedicated to reliability. Headline items are regional
failover for the EU cluster, zero-downtime schema migrations, and a 99.95% SLO for
the query API. Feature work is intentionally throttled while error budgets recover.

## Next: workspace collaboration

The next quarter introduces shared workspaces: comment threads on saved queries,
role-based dashboards, and an approval flow for publishing changes to production
collections. A private beta opens to Team plan customers first.

## Later: on-prem and marketplace

The on-premises distribution, packaged as a Helm chart with an air-gapped installer,
is planned for the second half of the year. A partner marketplace for community
plugins is in design; revenue share terms are not final.

## How we prioritize

Roadmap items are scored by reach, impact, and effort, then reviewed monthly with
the customer advisory board. Committed items appear in the changelog with target
quarters; everything else is directional and may shift without notice.

## Requesting features

Feature requests go through the ideas portal. Duplicate requests are merged and
their vote counts combined. The top ten ideas by votes get a written status update
from the product team every quarter.
