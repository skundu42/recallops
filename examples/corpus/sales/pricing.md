# Pricing Overview

## Plans

The Free plan covers one project, community support, and a 14-day data retention
window. The Team plan is priced per seat with volume discounts at 25 and 100 seats,
adds SSO, longer retention, and priority support. Enterprise pricing is custom and
adds dedicated infrastructure options, audit logs, and a named account manager.

## Usage components

Beyond seats, bills include usage components: indexed document volume, query
volume above the plan allowance, and premium model calls. Usage is metered daily
and invoiced monthly in arrears. Unused allowance does not roll over.

## Discounts

Annual prepayment earns a 15% discount over monthly billing. Registered nonprofits
and accredited educational institutions qualify for 30% off Team plans. Startup
program members under two years old and under 2 million in funding get the Team
plan free for one year.

## Trials and downgrades

Team plan trials run 14 days with no credit card required and convert to Free
automatically if not upgraded. Downgrading mid-term takes effect at the next
renewal; we do not issue partial credits for early downgrades, consistent with the
refund policy.

## Price changes

Price changes are announced at least 60 days in advance and never apply
mid-contract. Renewals within 12 months of a price increase are capped at a 10%
uplift for existing customers.
