# Support Tickets FAQ

## Severity levels

Severity 1 means production is down or data is at risk; target first response is 30
minutes around the clock. Severity 2 covers degraded production functionality with
a 4 business-hour response target. Severity 3 and 4 cover questions and cosmetic
issues with next-business-day responses.

## Escalation

To escalate a ticket, reply with the word ESCALATE in the subject or ask the
account manager. Escalation pages the on-duty support lead and adds the ticket to
the daily incident review. Abuse of severity 1 for routine questions may result in
reclassification.

## Business hours and coverage

Standard support operates weekdays 6:00 to 18:00 US Eastern. Enterprise plans add
24/7 coverage for severity 1 and 2. Holidays follow the published support calendar;
severity 1 coverage never pauses.

## Attachments and diagnostics

Tickets accept attachments up to 25 MB. For larger diagnostics bundles, request an
upload link and we provision a scoped bucket valid for 7 days. Never attach API
keys or customer PII; redact logs before uploading.

## Satisfaction surveys

Every resolved ticket sends a two-question CSAT survey. Scores below 3 out of 5
trigger a review by the support manager within one business day.
